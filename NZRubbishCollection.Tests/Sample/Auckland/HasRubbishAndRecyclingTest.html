Where you can put your rubbish, food scraps and recycling for collection

Household rubbish

Put your rubbish bin out on the kerbside by 7am on your collection day, or the night before.

All properties are supplied with one red lidded rubbish bin for household rubbish.

Food scraps

This property is listed as out of food scraps service area and will not receive collection service.

Household recycling

Put items that can be recycled in the council supplied yellow-lidded bin and place the bin on the kerbside before 7am on your collection day, or the night before.

The bin is the property of Auckland Council and needs to stay with this property if you move.

These bins cannot be used for commercial quantities of recycling.

Commercial rubbish

'

Put your bin out to the kerbside before 7am on your collection day, or the night before.

This service may not be suitable for commercial properties that generate large quantities of rubbish.

Commercial recycling

Put items that can be recycled in the council supplied yellow-lidded bin and place the bin on the kerbside before 7am on your collection day, or the night before.

The bin is the property of Auckland Council and needs to stay with this property if you move.

These bins cannot be used for commercial quantities of recycling.
